Enemrgency Evacuation Analysis

In this Project , I conducted an emergency evacuation analysis for a potential rail chemical spill scenario in London. Utilizing ArcGIS Pro, I created evacuation zones based on the proximity to railway tracks, distinguishing between mandatory and voluntary evacuation areas. By analyzing building, dwelling, and population counts within these zones, I assessed potential risks and estimated the population affected in each evacuation zone. Additionally, I developed a custom Process Modeling Tool for spatial analysis and symbology to streamline the analysis process. Through this project, I enhanced my proficiency in GIS spatial analysis, data interpretation, and cartographic representation, gaining valuable insights into emergency response planning and spatial risk assessment.
