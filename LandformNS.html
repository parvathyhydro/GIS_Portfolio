Landform Of Nova Scotia

In this project, I embarked on a journey to recreate a map of Nova Scotia akin to one crafted by Peter Atwood (2021) using ArcGIS Pro and open data sources. Through meticulous attention to detail, I honed my skills in advanced symbology techniques, including custom symbol creation and color ramp design. By meticulously crafting layouts and incorporating elements like annotations and legends, I produced a high-quality map suitable for professional presentation.
