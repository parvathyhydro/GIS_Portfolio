Python Programming for Dredging Management in ArcGIS Pro

In this project, I developed geoprocessing functionalities and data management procedures in ArcGIS Pro using Python. Firstly, I addressed the Ministry of the Environment's mandate for the Catfish Creek Conservation Authority to manage dredging operations independently. I created a Script Tool to split channel lines based on soundings, crucial for assessing silt build-up. Additionally, I managed data in Python in Jupyter Notebooks for field use. I converted stream bank polygons to polylines, merged them with lateral lines, and generated polygons for mapping dredging areas. Furthermore, I automated field data entry by populating fields like ChannelSegId and Technician Name from Excel sheets. Finally, I published the edited feature layer to ArcGIS Online for collaborative use in contract negotiations and field operations, adhering to data standards and ensuring ease of access and update.
