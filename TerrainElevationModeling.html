Terrain Elevation Modeling

In completing this Project ,I meticulously crafted an academic poster showcasing my adeptness in terrain and elevation modeling. Leveraging raster data and geoprocessing tools, I meticulously processed and analyzed data sourced from Land Information Ontario and the City of London, focusing on the Thames River Valley in West London. With a keen eye for detail, I created raster digital elevation models from the provided data, employing interpolation techniques and surface analysis tools to generate comprehensive visualizations. Through a thoughtful combination of graphics and descriptive text, my poster elucidates the intricacies of DEM creation workflows, analysis of classified point cloud data, and three-dimensional raster visualization, while adhering to stringent word limits and including requisite copyright information. This project not only honed my technical skills but also sharpened my ability to effectively communicate complex geospatial concepts through concise and visually engaging means.
